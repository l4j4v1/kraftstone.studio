Spotify Bleend Project

Academic 2024

Brief of the project

The project focused on collaborating with a mobile app—Spotify—to promote its new feature, “Spotify Blend.” The goal was to explain how the feature works and create a social media promotional package to support its launch.

Promotional Video

Spotify Blend allows users to invite friends to share a dynamic playlist that updates daily with songs they both enjoy. It also features a “taste match” rating to compare musical compatibility. To communicate this in a fun and relatable way, I created an animated promotional video with a party theme—highlighting how music naturally brings people together in social settings like parties, where connections often happen through shared sound.

Social Media Videos

I also created short videos designed for Instagram to invite users to try out Spotify’s new feature, making the launch more engaging and shareable across social media.

Final Result

This project resulted in a vibrant and engaging promotional campaign for Spotify’s "Blend" feature, combining storytelling, animation, and social media strategy. Through a party-themed animated video, the feature’s function—automatically generating shared playlists between friends—was explained in a way that felt personal, fun, and socially relevant.

Tools & Softwares

Some of the tools and sotfwares I used to carry out this project were: Prototype (figma), Logo ( Adove Illustrator) and Promotional video ( After Effects).
